I will share about my recent trip to China through the theme of doing God’s work in His love, not ours.

 

Brief summary of my trip:

Shanghai – Fudan University conference
China – opening up more – Rick Warren’s book.

Shanghai – church buildings.

 

Theme scripture:

 

I Peter 1:22             Since by your obedience to the Truth (through the Holy Spirit), you have purified your hearts for the sincere affection of the brethren, see that you love one another fervently from a pure heart.

 

The attractive aspect of the Christian faith is not the miracles, signs and wonders (which humans like to see and always want God to perform), but it is doing the ordinary daily things in an extraordinary way.

 

Mother Teresa – beautiful example.  Do everything in LOVE.  God’s love.  One drop of oil causes light to burn from an oil lamp.

 

A little amount of resources and spaces produces a great amount of Godly ministry if done in God’s love.

 

POWERPOINT PRESENTATION – Jiaxing church outside of Shanghai.

 

Two or three quotes from Mother Teresa

 

At the end of our lives, God will not be concerned about how much we did but He will be concerned about whether or not we did it in agape love.  Acts done in agape love, God’s love, transform us into something He wants us to become in the process.  Acts done in human love or motives can easily tire us out.

 

Apostle Peter is a good example – I will DO all these things for you Lord…

The resurrected Jesus came back to Peter and had to still ask Him, “Do you love me Peter?”  Is Jesus asking you and me that today in our busyness to serve Him?

 

Do we allow others to love us?  If you have difficulty in allowing others to love and serve you then you must ask, “Do I allow God to really love me in the way He wants to love me or do I only accept from God what I think is best?”  Reflect and mediate on this.

 

Do you impose your value system on God or do you allow God to teach you His value system?

 

Chinese culture is a very strong culture (i.e. saving face etc.)

 

POWERPOINT PRESENTATION – House church believers in China – Heilongjiang area.

 

They are doing many great works for the Lord but within the House church community, there is great division over the idea of “once saved, always saved” or what some people would call, eternal security.  Major doctrinal problem amongst them.

 

Hebrew 9:12-13; 10:26            If you continually and willfully sin you are not walking in faith or are in step with the Spirit.  Dead works and fruits and flesh.  So one is living under an illusion regarding one’s view of God.

 

Gal.5:22-26             Fruits of the Spirit.

 

I Thess.5:23b             …may your spirit and soul and body be preserved sound and complete [and found] blameless at the coming of our Lord Jesus Christ, the Messiah.
